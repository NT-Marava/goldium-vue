Keys to small scale Zimbabwean Mining.

Getting Prospecting License

The prospecting license is given in a specific province of the country at the ministry of mines

Prospect

The prospecting license is given in a specific province of the country at the ministry of mines

Find an Agent/Pegger

This is an experienced personnel who can identify the place on ministry maps and put a physical peg on the site

Apply for Registration

Submit an application for registration to the ministry and when approved, you can start mining activities.

Erect Beacons

In 3 months after approval you should erect permanent beacons on your claim.

Terms And Conditions

Claims have a tenure of 12 Months after which they shall expire or be renewed.
